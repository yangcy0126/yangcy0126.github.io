Cunyi YANG 杨存奕

Lingnan College

Sun Yat-sen University

Hello, I am Cunyi!

I am an active scholar in Finance at Lingnan College of Sun Yat-sen University, focusing on the Theory and Application of Risk Management in Digital Transformation. I believe that while the continuous advancement of digital technologies is certain, another key to ensuring they bring happiness to people is to clearly recognize their risks and manage them.

I have published several papers in academic journals, including Financial Review, Technological Forecasting & Social Change, Business Strategy and the Environment in English, as well as 经济研究 and 系统工程理论与实践 in Chinese.

OTHER ACTIVITIES

WDSI (War-Related Diplomatic Sentiment Index) is a high-frequency geopolitical sentiment dataset built from official diplomatic texts (15 countries, 2001–2026) using a multi-stage LLM pipeline. It supports cross-country and event-based analysis and predicts geopolitical risk and international capital flows.
