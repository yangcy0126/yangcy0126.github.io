Cunyi YANG 杨存奕

HKU Business School

The University of Hong Kong

Hello, I am Cunyi!

I am a URC Postdoctoral Fellow at HKU Business School, The University of Hong Kong, and hold a Ph.D. in Finance from Sun Yat-sen University. My work has appeared in Journal of Banking and Finance, Financial Review, Journal of Business Research, and Technological Forecasting and Social Change, among others. I serve as an Assistant Editor of International Review of Finance.

I have received the Job Market Star recognition at the China Economics Annual Conference, the China Excellent Young Scholar Paper Award in Finance, and the Top-Ranked Paper distinction in the 13th Guangdong Provincial Award for Excellence in Financial Research.

OTHER ACTIVITIES

Digital Economics

It is a WeChat public account that Cunyi has operated independently since 2023. It shares current topics, academic literature, calls for papers, and policy updates related to digital economics and digital finance. To date, it has attracted over 8,000 followers, published 140+ original articles, and accumulated over 300,000 total reads.

Diplomatic Sentiment Index

It is a high-frequency geopolitical sentiment dataset built from official diplomatic texts from 15 countries between 2001 and 2026 using a multi-stage LLM pipeline. It supports cross-country and event-based analysis and helps predict geopolitical risk and international capital flows.
