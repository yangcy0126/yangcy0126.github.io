WDSI (War-Related Diplomatic Sentiment Index) Observatory is a research data platform developed by Cunyi. It constructs high-frequency geopolitical sentiment indices from official diplomatic texts across 9 countries (2001–2026). Using a multi-stage LLM pipeline, the project identifies war-related content, classifies sentiment, and aggregates signals into daily indices with smoothing. The platform provides cross-country comparisons, event-aligned time series, and open-access datasets. The index predicts geopolitical risk dynamics and international capital flows.
