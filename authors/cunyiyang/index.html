Cunyi YANG 杨存奕

HKU Business School

The University of Hong Kong

I am a URC Postdoctoral Fellow at HKU Business School, The University of Hong Kong, and hold a Ph.D. in Finance from Sun Yat-sen University. My work has appeared in Journal of Banking and Finance, Financial Review, Journal of Business Research, and Technological Forecasting and Social Change, among others. I serve as an Assistant Editor of International Review of Finance.

I have received the Job Market Star recognition at the China Economics Annual Conference, the China Excellent Young Scholar Paper Award in Finance, and the Top-Ranked Paper distinction in the 13th Guangdong Provincial Award for Excellence in Financial Research.

Research Interests

  • Digital finance and risk management
  • Digital transformation and economic welfare
  • FinTech, energy markets, and climate risk
